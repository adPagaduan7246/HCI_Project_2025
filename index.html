Courses We Offer

Here are some of the examples you could learn in this program!

Introduction in Computing

Learn the basic and enhance your understanding of programming. Focusing on the fundamentals and what works underneath the visual. Study history, theories and more.

Various Elective Classes

Choose your elective classes in our vast pool of choices. Ranging on designing your own system to training an Artificial Intelligence Models to building your own Robots.

Learn basic skills to build system and communicate with your stakeholders, training you to be able to tackle any problems with proper guidelines and techniques.
